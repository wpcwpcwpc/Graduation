Reviewer 14 of SMC 2021 submission 250

Comments to the author
======================

This paper proposed an attention-based residual network
structure and applied it to the pyramid scene
parsing network model. The model can infer the attention
map in turn along the channel and spatial dimensions, then
multiply the attention-map by the input feature map and
input it to the residual network structure. The authors
validated their model through experiments on data sets
obtained and labeled from Beijing Childrens Hospital. 

The experimental part is strong but the novelty of the
proposed method could be further reinforced.

The authors are suggested to better define "lightness".
本文提出了一种基于注意力的残差网络
结构并将其应用于金字塔场景
解析网络模型。 该模型可以推断注意力
依次沿通道和空间维度映射，然后
将注意力图乘以输入特征图和
将其输入到残差网络结构中。 作者
通过对数据集的实验验证了他们的模型
从北京儿童医院获得并标记。

实验性强但新颖
建议的方法可以进一步加强。

建议作者更好地定义“亮度”。
